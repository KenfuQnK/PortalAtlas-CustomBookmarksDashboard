    Settings

    Google Drive

    Optional

    Sync Portal Atlas with your Google Drive account to enjoy better features:

    • Sync your custom bookmarks across all your devices.
    • Keep a sharper copy of each image.
    • Create and save more customized cards.

    Google Chrome extensions have very limited local storage. Therefore, synchronizing with Google Drive is recommended because it greatly expands storage capacity.

    Portal Atlas only stores a private copy of your custom images in your Drive and has no access to the rest of your personal Google Drive files.

    This build still needs its publisher OAuth Client ID before Drive can be connected.
    Not connected Portal Atlas works normally without Drive.
